HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID 19

Made with love by Prograd Junior, Sanskriti Amidesh

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30 seconds will help reduce germs.

Cover Your Mouth & Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public. Even better, use a mask.

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or going to crowded places. Prefer staying indoors.

Eat Healthy

Avoid eating junk food. Eat as healthy as possible to boost your immunity.

Be Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to keep yourself calm and composed.

Be Aware...Be Safe...
